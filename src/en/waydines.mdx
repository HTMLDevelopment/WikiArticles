**Waydines** (/we&#618;da&#618;nɛs/), officially the **Parliamentary Republic of Waydines**, is an 
[elysian](Elysium) and a [brazilbourgish](Brazilburg) country (which makes it a cousin-nation of 
[Frigustell](Frigustell)). Composed of two districts, being these its planned capital [Mitterfurt](Mitterfurt) 
and the city of [Gremont](Gremont), it has a total of 13 officially registered citizens (2024). 
Adittionally, Waydines is a member and founder state of the [Elysian Union](Elysian%20Union).
